Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DownloadMedia.

What formats and codecs are supported?

We support pretty much everything you could need! On the video side, you can save files as MP4, WebM, MKV, MOV, FLV, and AVI. For audio-only lovers, we offer MP3, M4A, FLAC, and WAV. When it comes to codecs, you have the standard H.264 for maximum compatibility, H.265 (HEVC) and AV1 for insane compression without quality loss, plus heavy-duty professional editing formats like Apple ProRes 422 and DNxHR HQ. Basically, whatever you need, we've got you covered.

Are there any speed limits on downloads?

Absolutely not. Unlike a lot of other tools or websites that throttle your speeds to sell you a premium plan, DownloadMedia uses multi-threaded pipelines to max out your internet connection. You get the file as fast as your provider and the source servers allow. If that's too fast and it's slowing down your browsing, you can easily throttle it yourself in the settings.

Does it bypass DRM?

No, it doesn't. DownloadMedia respects the encryption (DRM) used by commercial streaming services like Netflix, Hulu, or Disney+. The app is strictly built for archiving fair-use content from social media platforms, video sharing sites, and open public streams.

Is DownloadMedia really free?

Yes, it is 100% free forever. There are no paywalls, no 'premium' subscriptions, no hidden limits, and definitely no ads stuffed into the app. It's a tool built by the community, for the community.

Can I download an entire playlist or channel?

You bet! Just paste the URL of a YouTube playlist, and the app will grab the whole queue for you. You can sit back while it extracts everything in the highest quality. We even have a built-in queue system so you can see exactly which video is currently downloading, how big the files are, and the estimated time remaining.

Why should I use this instead of a website downloader?

Those free downloader websites are notoriously sketchy. They're often packed with intrusive popup ads, malware redirects, and strict limits on video length or quality (they rarely let you download true 4K without paying). Since DownloadMedia runs natively on your desktop, you get a clean, ad-free experience, unlimited 4K/8K downloads, and the absolute best codecs directly from the source.

How does the proxy feature work?

If you're trying to archive a video that happens to be geo-blocked or restricted in your region, we've got a neat workaround. You can head into the settings and paste a custom HTTP or SOCKS5 proxy. The app will securely route all extraction traffic through that proxy, letting you bypass those pesky regional walls effortlessly.

What's the best format for video editing?

If you plan on dropping the video right into Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, or After Effects, you'll definitely want to use Apple ProRes 422 or DNxHR HQ. They are 'intra-frame' codecs, which means they produce massive file sizes, but they require almost zero processing power to scrub through your timeline. No more laggy editing!

Is DownloadMedia a desktop application?

DownloadMedia runs natively as a desktop application on Windows, macOS, and Linux. This allows us to provide maximum download speeds, support high-quality 4K/8K codecs, and ensure a secure, ad-free experience.

Can I download videos for free?

DownloadMedia.me allows users to download videos online for free. DownloadMedia.me supports high-quality video downloads and conversions.

Does it work on mobile?

No, DownloadMedia is currently only available for desktop operating systems (Windows, macOS, and Linux) to run native extraction tools locally.

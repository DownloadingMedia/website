Getting Started

Download anything in three simple steps.

01

How to Download a Video or Audio

Paste any video or audio link into the app. DownloadMedia resolves metadata instantly. Select Video or Audio, pick your desired format and codec, and hit Download File. For batch downloads, paste a playlist link.

02

Which App/Editor Supports Which Codec?

Web Players & Browsers: Prefer H.264 (AVC) and MP4.
Adobe Premiere Pro: Prefers Apple ProRes 422 or H.264.
Adobe After Effects: Heavily prefers Apple ProRes or Avid DNxHR HQ for scrubbing performance.
DaVinci Resolve: Excellent with DNxHR HQ and GoPro CineForm.

03

Best Quality Formats & Extensions

Video Archival: MKV with H.265 (HEVC) or AV1 offers the absolute best quality-to-size ratio.
Maximum Compatibility: MP4 with H.264 is universally supported.
Audio Audiophiles: FLAC (lossless) is best, but 320kbps MP3 or M4A offers transparent quality at a fraction of the size.

04

Ideal Download Configurations

For general offline viewing, we recommend 1080p + H.264 (MP4). For 4K archival of YouTube content, choose 4K + VP9/AV1 (WebM/MKV). If you intend to edit the footage immediately, always select Apple ProRes 422 (MOV) to save your CPU during playback.

05

Using Proxies for Geo-Restricted Content

If a video is blocked in your country, navigate to the Settings page. Under "Network & Engine", enter an HTTP or SOCKS5 proxy URL (e.g., socks5://127.0.0.1:1080). DownloadMedia will route all extraction traffic through this proxy, bypassing regional blocks safely.

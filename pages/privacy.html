Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Applies to DownloadMedia desktop application and this website

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains exactly what data DownloadMedia collects, why it is collected, which third-party services may receive it, how long it is retained, and what rights you have over it. We have written this to be honest and readable — not to bury things in legal language.

1. What Data We Collect

DownloadMedia is a local desktop application. It does not require account registration, does not upload your downloaded files to any server, and does not track your usage by default. The data we collect is minimal and falls into two categories:

Data Type What It Is Why We Collect It
Download history URLs you paste and files you download Stored locally on your device only — so the app can show your history. Never sent to our servers.
App settings & preferences Format choices, output folder path, quality settings Stored locally on your device only — to remember your preferences between sessions.
Crash reports (if enabled) Stack trace, OS version, app version, memory details, uptime, and last 50 debug logs Sent directly to our private Discord logging channel via secure webhook. Contains no personal identifiers (like name/IP), but debug logs may contain download URLs processed during the crash. Disabled by default.
Update checks Your current app version, OS platform, and architecture Sent to Google Drive API and GitHub raw content servers to verify if a newer update is available and display release notes. No personal info is logged.
Website visit data Browser type, referral source, pages visited Collected anonymously by website analytics (if any) to understand visitor traffic. No personal identification.

We do not collect: your name, email address, phone number, payment details, location data, contacts, camera or microphone access, biometric data, or any information that directly identifies you as an individual.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not upload, scan, or analyse files you download
  • We do not collect or store the URLs you paste unless you save them to your local history
  • We do not read your cookies, browser history, or session tokens from other applications
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers
  • We do not use your data to build advertising profiles

3. Third-Party Services That May Receive Data

The Application interacts with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Discord — if opted-in to crash reports, logs and system stats are transmitted directly to a private Discord channel managed by the DownloadMedia team. Discord Privacy Policy
  • Google Drive API — used by the desktop application to check for updates and retrieve setup binaries. Google Privacy Policy
  • IMDb Suggestion API — queried by the application's search bar during Movie or TV show lookups to retrieve metadata. IMDb Privacy Policy
  • GitHub — used to fetch the official release changelogs and distribute code assets. GitHub Privacy Policy
  • CounterAPI — used on this website to display and increment global download counts. CounterAPI Policy
  • yt-dlp extraction targets (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.) — these platforms receive standard network requests when the app parses media links. Your interaction with those platforms is governed by their own Terms and Privacy Policies.
  • Google Fonts (website only) — fonts are loaded from Google's servers when you visit this website. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics (website only) — we use Google Analytics to measure website traffic, understand how visitors interact with our site, and improve the user experience. The data collected is anonymised and aggregated. Google Privacy Policy

4. How Long Data Is Retained

  • Local app data (download history, settings) — retained indefinitely on your device. You can clear it at any time from the app's Settings page.
  • Crash reports — retained indefinitely in our private Discord logging channel unless deleted or purged by channel administrators.
  • Website analytics — aggregated and anonymised. No personally identifiable data is retained.

5. Your Rights Over Your Data

You have full control over your locally stored data at all times. You can:

  • Access — view all locally stored data via the app's Settings > Data section
  • Delete — clear your download history and reset all preferences from Settings > Clear Data
  • Uninstall — uninstalling the application removes all locally stored app data from your device
  • Opt out of crash reporting — disable this at any time from Settings > Privacy

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. If you are in California, you have rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via our Discord server .

6. Data Security

We follow these security practices to protect your data:

  • All network communications from the application use HTTPS/TLS
  • No passwords are stored — the Application does not have user accounts
  • Crash reports are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections directly to a private Discord channel managed by the DownloadMedia team
  • We do not store sensitive session cookies — any cookies you provide (via `cookies.txt` or settings) to bypass age-gates are stored locally on your device only and never uploaded

7. Children's Privacy

DownloadMedia is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it. The Application should only be used by individuals aged 18 and above, as stated in our Terms of Service.

8. Cookie Policy (This Website)

This website does not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies. If cookies are used, they are strictly limited to:

  • Functional cookies — to remember your preferences on this website (e.g. dark/light mode toggle, if implemented)
  • Analytics cookies — anonymised, aggregate visit statistics. No personal data is attached.

We do not use cross-site tracking cookies, remarketing pixels, or fingerprinting scripts.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the Application after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Significant changes — such as us beginning to collect new categories of data — will be communicated via a notice in the Application or on our Discord server.

10. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to your privacy or this policy, please contact us through:

  • Our official Discord server

We aim to respond to all privacy-related requests within 30 days.

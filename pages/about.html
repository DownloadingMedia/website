About Me

No corporate jargon. Just a developer building a cleaner web.

Hey there! Welcome to DownloadMedia. I am super happy you visited this page to know more about me and this project.

My name is Megh, and I am the developer of DownloadMedia. I love coding and building useful tools. To be honest, this project started out of pure frustration. Like everyone else, I frequently needed to download videos, audio files, or reels from the internet (maybe to show a funny video to my family offline, or to extract high-quality footage for some video editing work).

But every time I searched for an online downloader, it was a nightmare. You open a site, and boom, three popups block your screen, adult ads start flashing, and redirect loops try to install suspicious extensions on your browser. Even if the download starts, it throttles your speed or restricts HD downloads unless you pay. It felt shady and frustrating.

The Weekend Decision

One Saturday evening, over a cup of hot cutting chai, I thought: "Why is downloading a simple file so complicated and dirty? The underlying command-line tools to download media are open-source and run perfectly. Why can't I just build a beautiful, clean, native desktop application that wraps these tools and gives people an ad-free experience?"

So I spent my weekends coding the first version. My goal was simple:

  • No ads or popups whatsoever.
  • No artificial speed limits.
  • Complete privacy: no tracking or telemetry. Your downloads are processed directly on your machine.
  • Completely free to use.

Why a Desktop App instead of a Website?

Websites require expensive servers to process downloads, which forces creators to show annoying popups and sell user data to pay the bills. By packing DownloadMedia as a native app (for Windows, Mac, and Linux), the extraction runs locally using your own computer's processor and internet bandwidth. This keeps my running costs close to zero and guarantees your data never leaves your system.

How I Keep It Running

Because the app runs locally, I don't have massive server bills. However, maintaining the software, paying for domain names, and web hosting still costs money. I build this in my spare time and have no corporate backing.

To help pay these domain bills and keep this project alive, I have placed Google AdSense ads on this website. Please note that the desktop application itself is and will always remain completely ad-free. Only this static website displays ads. These are standard Google ads, which are safe and verified, and they motivate me to continue developing and updating the app.

If you like using DownloadMedia, that is more than enough for me. But if you want to support me, you can buy me a cup of tea (or cutting chai!) by using the Donate button, or join our Discord community to say hello and tell me what features I should build next.

Thanks for reading,
Megh
Developer of DownloadMedia

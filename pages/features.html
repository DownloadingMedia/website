{ Core Features }

Built for Speed & Control

Everything you need to download, stream, and archive media from anywhere — in one clean desktop app.

1

Multi-threaded Speeds

Powered by native yt-dlp pipelines with concurrent streams, adjustable speed limits, and socket timeouts for stable connections.

2

Direct Link Player

Play downloaded media instantly in a custom built-in player with keyboard hotkeys, volume control, and playback speed.

3

Extensive Video Formats

Download native videos in MP4, MOV, WebM, MKV, FLV, and AVI. Choose the exact container format you need for archival or playback.

4

Lossless Audio Extraction

Extract ultra-high-quality audio streams natively in MP3, M4A, WAV, and FLAC at up to 320kbps bitrates.

5

Modern & Native Codecs

Support for modern delivery codecs like H.264 (AVC), H.265 (HEVC), VP9, and AV1. Easily export with hardware acceleration for maximum quality and compression.

6

Editor-Ready Presets

Instantly transcode to intermediate codecs for professional editing: Apple ProRes 422 (Proxy, LT, Standard, HQ, 4444), Avid DNxHR HQ, and GoPro CineForm.

7

Cookies Manager & Proxies

Bypass age-restrictions and paywalls securely via session cookies (Netscape format) and customizable HTTP/SOCKS5 proxy networking.

8

Batch & Queues

Queue multiple items or extract entire playlists. Track speed, progress, file size, and ETA in real time.

9

Adaptive Theme

Detects OS light/dark mode and dynamically adjusts toolbar icon colors for perfect visibility at all times.
